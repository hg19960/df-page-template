Who are we??

Founded in 1992, Dhami Finance is the financial arm of Dhami Group ©. We are an RBI registered NBFC. We have specialized in real estate and vehicle debt financing for the last 29 years. The upswing in the technology sector due to work-from-home and remote communication requirements drove us towards directing investment towards Fin-Tech platforms. We strive to achieve expertise in that sector of lending. At Dhami Finance we believe in growing with others hence we are open to business collaborations with external stakeholders.
